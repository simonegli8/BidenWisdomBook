About Us

Just 3 People with a Common Passion

Greetings from Hartland, Wisconsin!

We are Art, Michelle, and Steve. We believe in hope. In change. In progress. We believe that our moment is now.

We are dreamers who believe that in today’s busy and distracting world, brief, to the point and thought-provoking Quotations can ignite ideas and ideals, leading people to Act.

Our dream is that our simple yet powerful idea and website will inspire you; that our instant free books with the compiled Words, Wit, and Wisdom of Barack Obama on issues important to you will move you and others to think, talk, believe, and Vote.

Coffee tables in homes, offices, and waiting rooms throughout America will have ObamaWisdomBook.com free books ready to be picked up and spark a conversation about Barack Obama’s Change We Can Believe In.
