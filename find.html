Find Quotes on Issues You Care About

Simple Instructions for using this page
  1. Click on the issues you and your fellow citizens are interested in.
  2. Then click to read Joe Biden’s quote on the topic.
  3. To create your own free coffee table book of Joe Biden quotes, check the box next to each quote you want to include in your book.
  4. Then, simply click the “Create My Book” button at the bottom of the page.
  5. Last but not least, slip your printed sheets into sheet protectors and assemble your book in a view binder. For more suggestions, check out “Spread the Words“.
