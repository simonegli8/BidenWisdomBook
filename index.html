We are Art, Michelle, and Steve, 3 "grass-rooters" from Wisconsin, with a vision as improbable as Barack Obama's candidacy: enough books on enough coffee tables so that every American has direct access to the words, wit, and wisdom of Barack Obama.

We have been forever changed by this election, and we look forward to a brighter tomorrow with hope in our hearts. Thank you, America.

As the world has learned during the course of this campaign, Barack Obama is arguably one of the most eloquent, inspirational, and transformational leaders of our time. Hear him speak and you'll believe in the possibilities for our future. Read his words and understand how real hope can change the world.

We created ObamaWisdomBook.com to fill a need during this past election. Americans needed access to Barack Obama's quotes - unfiltered and undistorted - in order to make an informed decision in the voting booth.

Now that the election is over, the need is even greater. We each must continue to care, continue to hope, and continue to participate in the future of our country. And a large part of doing that is to hold President-elect Obama accountable to what we as a people want OUR America to become.
